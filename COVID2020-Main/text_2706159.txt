The novel coronavirus, or COVID-19, is obviously a serious situation for some regions in the world. But for some reason, the first thing people do when there's a crisis in the news is run to the nearest grocery store to hoard toilet paper, canned beans, and bread. Meanwhile the US Surgeon General is begging people to stop buying masks and yuppies in Brooklyn are raiding Trader Joe's.
