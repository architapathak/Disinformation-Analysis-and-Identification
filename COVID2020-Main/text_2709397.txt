BANGKOK (Reuters) - Thailand has reported one new coronavirus case, bringing the total number of such cases in the country to 43 since January, a senior health official said on Monday. The new case is a 22-year-old Thai woman who works with another Thai patient, a Thai driver for foreign tourists, Sukhum Kanchanaphimai, the permanent secretary of the health ministry, said in a news conference. So far 31 coronavirus patients have recovered and returned home and 11 are still being treated in hospitals. Thailand recorded its first coronavirus fatality on Sunday.
