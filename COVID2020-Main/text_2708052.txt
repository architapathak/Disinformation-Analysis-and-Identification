DUBAI, March 5 (Reuters) - A major aerospace summit in Abu Dhabi this month, which was due to be attended by Saab President Micael Johansson and Thales International President Pascale Sourisse among others, has been postponed until June because of the coronavirus, according to an email sent to participants and seen by Reuters. The March 17-19 Global Aerospace Summit will now take place from June 8 to 10, the email says. Organisers did not immediately respond to a request for comment. (Reporting by Alexander Cornwell; Editing by Susan Fenton)
