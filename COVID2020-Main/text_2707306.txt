BEIJING (Reuters) - Authorities will ease restrictions in Wuhan, the center of the coronavirus outbreak which is still in lockdown, when situation improves, a senior government official said on Friday. Ding Xiangyang, Vice Secretary General at the State Council, told a media briefing in Wuhan that winter had passed and the day everyone was looking forward to would not be far away.
