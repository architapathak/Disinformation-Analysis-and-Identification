AMSTERDAM, March 6 (Reuters) - Dutch airline KLM on Friday said it would cancel several flights to Italy in the coming week because of the widespread coronavirus outbreak in the country. The Dutch arm of airline group Air France KLM will cancel two daily flights to Milan and Rome, one daily flight to Bologna and one daily flight to Venice from March 7 until March 15 at least.
