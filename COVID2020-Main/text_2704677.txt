The Senate Commerce, Science and Transportation Committee is set to hold a hearing on the aviation industry and its role in containing the coronavirus outbreak.  Officials from the Department of Transportation, Centers for Disease Control and Prevention and U.S. Customs and Border Protection are set to testify before the panel.
