SHANGHAI/BEIJING, March 1 (Reuters) - Mainland China reported 573 new confirmed coronavirus cases on Feb. 29, up from 427 on the previous day, the country’s health authority said on Sunday. The number of deaths stood at 35, down from 47 on the previous day, bringing the total death toll in mainland China to 2,870. Of the deaths, 34 were in Hubei province, the epicentre of the outbreak. The province also saw 570 of the new cases. (Reporting by David Stanway and Hallie Gu, Editing by Rosalba O’Brien)
