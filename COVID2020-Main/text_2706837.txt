ROME, March 4 (Reuters) - The Italian government is considering closing schools all over the country due to its coronavirus outbreak, but no decision has been taken yet, a government source said on Wednesday. At the moment only schools in the northern regions most affected by the epidemic are closed. Seventy-nine people had died of the highly contagious illness in Italy up to Tuesday, with more than 2,500 confirmed cases. Reporting by Giuseppe Fonte, writing by Francesca Piscioneri,
