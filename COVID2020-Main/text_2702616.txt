BERLIN, March 2 (Reuters) - Germany must not allow the coronavirus outbreak to infect its economic growth, Economy Minister Peter Altmaier told ARD public broadcaster on Monday. "That's what we're talking about in the government," Altmaier added. Asked about economic stimulus proposals by Finance Minister Olaf Scholz, Altmaier said he was open to tax incentives and enhanced write-down measures for companies.
