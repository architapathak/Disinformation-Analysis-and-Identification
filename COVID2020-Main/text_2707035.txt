March 5 (Reuters) - Canada’s main stock index fell at the open on Thursday, as worries of coronavirus rose following an increase in the death toll in the United States due to the epidemic. * At 9:30 a.m. ET (1430 GMT), the Toronto Stock Exchange’s S&P/TSX composite index was down 275.77 points, or 1.64%, at 16,503.76.
