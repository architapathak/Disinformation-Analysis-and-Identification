SANTIAGO (Reuters) - Chile recorded the first confirmed case of coronavirus in the country, the health ministry said on Tuesday. The patient is a 33-year-old man in the city of Talca, south of Santiago. Neighboring Argentina also confirmed its first case on Tuesday, a 43-year-old man who had traveled to Italy.
