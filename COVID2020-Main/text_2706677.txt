WASHINGTON, March 4 (Reuters) - The head of the U.S. House of Representatives committee in charge of spending expects the entire chamber to take up an emergency bill on the coronavirus on Wednesday evening or Thursday morning at the latest. House Appropriations Committee Chairwoman Nita Lowey said on Wednesday the bill would total “a little over” $8 billion. (Reporting by David Morgan; Writing by Lisa Lambert, Editing by Franklin Paul)
