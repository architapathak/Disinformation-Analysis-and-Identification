BRATISLAVA (Reuters) - Slovakia has its first case of the coronavirus, Prime Minister Peter Pellegrini said on Friday. Pellegrini said the 52-year-old patient was at a Bratislava hospital with mild symptoms. The man had not travelled abroad recently but his son, who had shown no symptoms, had been in Venice, Pellegrini said. Reporting by Tomas Mrva; editing by John Stonestreet
