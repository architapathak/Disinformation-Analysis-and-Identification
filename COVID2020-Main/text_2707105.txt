GENEVA, March 2 (Reuters) - The epidemics in South Korea, Italy, Iran and Japan are the World Health Organization’s greatest concern, the body’s chief Tedros Adhanom Ghebreyesus said on Monday. In the past 24 hours there were almost nine times more coronavirus cases reported outside China than inside, but the disease can be contained with the right measures, he said.
