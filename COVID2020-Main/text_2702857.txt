March 2 (Reuters) - Visa Inc said on Monday it expects its second-quarter revenue growth to be 2.5-3.5 percentage points lower than its previous forecast, due to the coronavirus outbreak. On its first-quarter earnings call in January, Visa had said it expects second-quarter net revenue growth to be in the low double digits, compared with the first quarter. (Reporting by Bharath Manjesh in Bengaluru; Editing by Krishna Chandra Eluri)
