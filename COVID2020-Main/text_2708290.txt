KARACHI, Pakistan, March 3 (Reuters) - Pakistan on Tuesday reported a new coronavirus case, bringing the total to five in the south Asian nation since last week, officials said. “We have now 5th confirmed case of COVID19 in federal areas,” Zafar Mirza, health minister said a in tweet early Tuesday morning. The patient is stable and being managed well, the minister added. (Reporting by Syed Raza Hassan; Editing by Kim Coghill)
