"Due to the coronavirus central banks are in a hurry to make fresh cuts and that is making ground for gold," said Vandana Bharti, assistant vice-president of commodity research at SMC Comtrade. "We were expecting a Fed rate cut in the second half of 2020 but we may see one in the first half."
