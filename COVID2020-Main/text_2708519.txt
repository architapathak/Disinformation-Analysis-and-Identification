PARIS, March 3 (Reuters) - France expects solidarity and flexibility from all European economic authorities on the coronavirus outbreak, Finance Minister Bruno Le Maire said on Tuesday. Le Maire also told a press conference it was important to consider all options including budgetary measures to deal with the economic impact of the coronavirus outbreak. “If there is a time we need to be flexible, it is when we are facing a crisis as important as the one we are facing today”, Le Maire said.
