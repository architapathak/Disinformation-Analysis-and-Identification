Neither Levin, a former environmental attorney, nor Maryott, a financial adviser and member of the San Juan Capistrano City Council, faced opposition in the primary. For both, the real fight is coming in November during the general election.Levin defeated his GOP challenger in 2018 by nearly 13 points.
