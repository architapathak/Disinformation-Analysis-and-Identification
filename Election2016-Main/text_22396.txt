A massive protest erupted in Manhattan over Donald Trump's victory in the presidential election on Tuesday. The throng of thousands began the protest at Union Square and marched up Broadway toward Trump Tower, halting rush hour traffic in its place. The mass of people spanned more than 40 city blocks.
